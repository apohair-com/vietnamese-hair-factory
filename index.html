Apohair's Hair Quality Tiers

At Apohair, we understand that beauty is not a one-size-fits-all concept. That's why we've created a range of hair quality tiers, each designed to cater to different preferences, budgets, and styling needs. Our offerings can be broadly categorized into three distinct levels: Standard, High Standard, and Luxury.

Standard Hair (S)

Our Standard hair collection is a testament to our commitment to quality at every price point. These extensions and wigs feature small, soft, and natural hair strands that closely mimic the texture and movement of your own tresses. While slightly more delicate than our higher tiers, Standard hair is still a cut above the rest, offering superior quality and longevity compared to many other options on the market.

Within the Standard hair category, we offer four sub-types – SA, SA+, SA++, and SA+++ – each with varying levels of tail thickness and ratios of short hair in each bundle. This diversity allows you to choose the perfect match for your desired look, whether you prefer a more subtle, natural finish or a slightly thicker, more voluminous style.

High Standard Hair (MS)

For those seeking a perfect balance between quality and affordability, our High Standard hair collection is an ideal choice. Boasting thicker strands than our Standard offerings, these extensions and wigs are designed for superior strength and durability, ensuring a longer-lasting, more resilient style.

One of the standout features of our High Standard hair is its slightly wavy texture post-washing, which helps to minimize frizz and tangling, making it a low-maintenance option for those with busy lifestyles. Additionally, this tier is available in a diverse array of colors, offering versatility to suit various preferences and skin tones.

Luxury Hair (L)

If you're in pursuit of the ultimate in hair quality, look no further than Apohair's Luxury hair collection. These extensions and wigs are crafted from the strongest, most resilient strands, with larger, thicker hair fibers that are virtually free of frizz and flyaways.

What truly sets our Luxury hair apart, however, is its unparalleled versatility when it comes to coloring and styling. Unlike many other hair products on the market, our Luxury hair can be easily bleached and dyed to achieve any desired shade, from radiant blondes to vibrant reds and everything in between.

Within the Luxury tier, we offer two sub-standards: LA2+ and LA3+, which differ in terms of the thickness of the hair ends, allowing you to customize your look with precision.
